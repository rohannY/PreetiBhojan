About Us

Shakunt Foods is a proprietory firm established in the year 2015 with a vision to serve Home Made, Hygienic Food to Corporate Employees. Since then, Preeti Bhojan (a Brand of Shakunt Foods) has become an epitome of Homely Cuisine. It is one of the promising names in the catering business and the food we serve is exceedingly popular among the people from all walks of life. Especially the employees from a number of industrial and commercial establishments prefer to have our food during working hours.

We serve Pure Veg food which comes with the following inherent qualities:

  • Made in Hygienic conditions with Farm Fresh Vegetables
  • Packaged in Food Grade, Leak Proof, Microwave Safe, Biodegradable Plastic Container
  • Lavish Menu
  • Reasonable Cost

We offer Premium Tiffin Service on a regular basis and leave no room for complaints on account of delay or unhygenic service. Our Proprietor Mrs. Preetisha Ruparel is a Master Chef and Backbone of our Business.
